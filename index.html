1. Privacy Policy
Last Updated: 19 April 2026

Atlas by KT is a global peer-to-peer trust platform operated by Kardashev Technologies ("we," "us," or "KT"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you use the Atlas by KT application and all associated features, including deal management, Trust Scores, wallet services, Atlas Hub listings, and the Operator programme (collectively, the "Platform"). By creating an account or using the Platform, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1.1 Who We Are

Atlas by KT is operated by Kardashev Technologies. We are a globally operating platform and this Policy applies to all users regardless of where they are located. Where local data protection laws impose additional requirements, we comply with those laws as described in Section 1.9 (Jurisdiction-Specific Provisions).

1.2 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the Platform:

  • Identity data: Full name, phone number, email address, profile photo, and your unique Atlas ID (ATX-XXXXX).
  • Account data: Username, preferred language, currency settings, and account configuration.
  • Transaction data: Deal history, deal amounts, deal status, counterparty ATX IDs, delivery OTP codes, and photo proof of delivery.
  • Wallet data: Available balance, pending balance, transaction records, withdrawal requests, and linked payout details.
  • Trust data: Trust Score, completed deal count, dispute history, and rank designation.
  • Location data: Approximate location used to surface nearby users on the Atlas Map, collected only with your explicit consent.
  • Device and usage data: IP address, device type, operating system, app version, and interaction logs.
  • Operator data: Atlas referral code, referral link activity, and commission records.
  • Support communications: Messages submitted through dispute resolution or customer support channels.

1.3 How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your Atlas account and ATX ID.
  • To facilitate peer-to-peer deals and payment protection.
  • To process OTP-based delivery confirmation and release funds accordingly.
  • To manage wallet balances, process withdrawals, and calculate Operator commissions.
  • To calculate and display Trust Scores and rank designations.
  • To verify identity via phone OTP and email authentication at account creation and key actions.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, and policy violations.
  • To display your approximate location on the Atlas Map when you have consented to this feature.
  • To send transactional communications including deal updates, OTP codes, and payout confirmations via push notification, SMS, or email.
  • To provide customer support and administer the dispute resolution process.
  • To improve the Platform using aggregated and anonymised analytics.
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations worldwide.

1.4 Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With deal counterparties: Your display name, ATX ID, and Trust Score are visible to the other party in a deal.
  • With Operators: If you registered via an Operator referral link, that Operator may see your referral status only — not your financial details or deal content.
  • With payment processors: Our integrated payment partners receive only the information necessary to process withdrawals and payouts.
  • With communication providers: SMS and email providers process the minimum data required to deliver messages to you.
  • With regulators and law enforcement: Where required by applicable law, court order, or to protect the safety and rights of users or third parties.
  • With successors: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred subject to equivalent privacy protections and prior notice to you.

1.5 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy. Financial and transaction records are retained for a minimum of seven (7) years to meet regulatory and compliance obligations. You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time, subject to these retention requirements.

1.6 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including: access to your data, correction of inaccurate data, deletion of your data, portability of your data, objection to certain processing, and withdrawal of consent. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@kardashevtechnologies.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may require identity verification before processing your request.

1.7 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including: encrypted data transmission (TLS), row-level database access controls, HMAC webhook verification, OTP-based deal confirmation, private storage with signed URLs, a validated deal state machine, and an immutable administrative audit log. No system is completely secure. If you suspect unauthorised access to your account, notify us immediately.

1.8 Children

Atlas by KT is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has registered an account, contact us immediately.

1.9 Jurisdiction-Specific Provisions

European Economic Area and United Kingdom users: We process your data under the lawful bases of contract performance, legal obligation, legitimate interests, and consent as applicable. You have rights under the GDPR and UK GDPR including the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Nigerian users: We process your data in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

California users: Under the CCPA and CPRA, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete it, and to opt out of its sale (we do not sell personal information).

Other jurisdictions: We comply with applicable local data protection laws in all markets where the Platform operates.

1.10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or by email prior to the changes taking effect. Continued use of the Platform after the updated Policy takes effect constitutes your acceptance.

  • Cancelled or mutually abandoned deals will be refunded to the buyer, minus any applicable fees.

3. Data & Compliance
Last Updated: 19 April 2026

This section sets out how Atlas by KT, operated by Kardashev Technologies, approaches data governance, regulatory compliance, and its obligations under applicable data protection laws across the jurisdictions in which the Platform operates. It applies to all personal data processed through the Platform, including data relating to Atlasians, Operators, and Admins.

3.1 Global Compliance Framework

Atlas by KT is a globally operating platform. We are committed to compliance with data protection and financial regulations across all markets in which we operate, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — European Economic Area users.
  • UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) — United Kingdom users.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) — California residents.
  • Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) — Nigerian users.
  • Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS) — as applicable to payment data handling via integrated processors.
  • Financial Action Task Force (FATF) recommendations on anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorism financing (CTF).
  • Applicable financial services regulations in each jurisdiction where withdrawals and payouts are processed.

3.2 Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal data only where a valid lawful basis exists:

  • Contract performance: Processing required to deliver deal management, wallet services, and account features you have contracted for.
  • Legal obligation: Processing required to comply with financial, tax, and regulatory obligations, and to respond to lawful authority requests.
  • Legitimate interests: Processing for fraud detection, Trust Score calculation, and Platform security, balanced against your rights and interests.
  • Consent: Processing for optional features such as location display on the Atlas Map. You may withdraw consent at any time.

3.3 Data Minimisation and Purpose Limitation

We collect only the minimum data necessary for the stated purposes described in our Privacy Policy. Data collected for one purpose is not repurposed for incompatible uses. We conduct regular reviews to ensure ongoing alignment with these principles.

3.4 Third-Party Data Processors

Atlas by KT engages the following categories of third-party processors under appropriate data processing agreements:

  • Database and storage: Supabase (PostgreSQL infrastructure; data encrypted at rest; access governed by row-level security policies).
  • SMS and OTP delivery: Twilio (processes phone numbers solely for authentication and deal confirmation messages).
  • Email delivery: Resend (processes email addresses solely for transactional communications).
  • Payment processing: Paystack, Flutterwave, and other integrated processors (operate within their own PCI-DSS-compliant environments; Atlas by KT does not store card numbers or sensitive payment credentials).

We do not transfer personal data to processors in jurisdictions without adequate data protection standards without implementing appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent mechanisms.

3.5 AML and Financial Compliance

As a platform facilitating financial transactions globally, Atlas by KT maintains a committed approach to anti-money laundering compliance:

  • Phone number verification via OTP is required at account creation.
  • Email verification is required for all registered accounts.
  • Transaction monitoring is in place for unusual patterns including high-value or high-frequency deal activity.
  • Enhanced identity verification is required for withdrawal requests above defined thresholds.
  • Financial transaction records are maintained for a minimum of seven (7) years.
  • We cooperate with financial intelligence authorities and law enforcement agencies as required by applicable law in each jurisdiction.

3.6 Technical Security Measures

  • All data transmitted between users and the Platform is encrypted via TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Row-level security (RLS) policies in our database restrict data access to authorised users only.
  • Webhook endpoints use HMAC signature verification to prevent unauthorised or spoofed requests.
  • OTP codes are single-use and time-limited, used for both authentication and deal confirmation.
  • Deal state transitions are enforced via a server-side validated state machine; no unauthorised transitions are permitted.
  • Delivery proof photos are stored in private paths and accessed only via signed URLs with expiry windows.
  • All administrative actions are recorded in an immutable audit log.
  • Idempotency controls via a processed events table prevent duplicate processing of webhook events.

3.7 Data Breach Response

In the event of a personal data breach likely to result in risk to individuals, we will notify relevant supervisory authorities within 72 hours where required, and notify affected users without undue delay where there is likely high risk to their rights. We maintain an internal incident response plan and conduct post-incident reviews.

3.8 User Rights Requests

To exercise your data rights, email info@kardashevtechnologies.com with the subject line 'Data Rights Request' and include your registered email address or ATX ID. We respond within 30 days. Identity verification may be required. Complex requests may be extended by a further 60 days with notice.
